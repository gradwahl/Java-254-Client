Treasure Trails Clues/Riddles Guide

Riddles are primarily to test your Runescape knowledge. If you remember most of the stuff you learned through quests and other information, you shouldn't have a problem with these. However, since there's one or two things that might have slipped your mind, here's a list of the current riddles:
